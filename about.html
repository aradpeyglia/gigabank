Our Story

Twenty years, one promise

2006

The first branch opens

Ahoura cashes in his savings and rents a 400 sq ft storefront on Telegraph Avenue.

2009

First 1,000 customers

Through the 2008 crisis, we never charged a single late fee. Word got around.

2013

Launched the mobile app

One of the first regional banks with mobile check deposit. It was a big deal back then.

2018

100 branches strong

Expansion across the West Coast. Every branch still gets a fresh-baked cookie tray.

2022

Crossed 1M customers

Named "Most Loved Bank" by Forbes for the first of three (and counting) years.

2026

2.4M members, 247 branches

And we still answer the phone on the second ring.
